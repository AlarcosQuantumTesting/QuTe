QuTe: Quantum Testing

About Quantum Test Suites

QuTe is a framework for designing and executing test suites on quantum circuits. Easily define deterministic test cases to verify exact state transitions, or stochastic test cases to validate expected probability distributions.

Powered by Qiskit Aer, QuTe automatically simulates your circuits and supports mutation analysis by injecting controlled logical faults to evaluate the robustness of your test suites.
